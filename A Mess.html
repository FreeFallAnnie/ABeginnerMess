The Dotted C

I started this painting with the intention of neatly colouring-in the dots.

Ha.

The page itself is from an old mindfulness diary, the ones where you practise breathing and calm by focussing on the work.

I cut out the C, out of curiosity and stuck it on the, then, white page - before setting out with the best of intentions.
