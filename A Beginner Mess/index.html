As a beginner there is a lot of opinions floating around. Personally I have seen beginner's punished for sharing their work.

To the point, I don't really see it on the internet... maybe Tumblr.... They seem nice.

But in general terms of social media, there is a preference for people to show the perfect. The undisputably "good" stuff. With AI up and about, the beginner artist, coder, learner - will look subpar.

Why should people care about sharing their imperfect work? Can't they keep it as their personal hobby?

True. People absolutely can...

and do.

But how much of life are we missing of people we care about? To only see the end product is to miss the entire journey. Which makes up their life.

Okay. But I can send messages to my friends and family, it doesn't have to be public.

Sure you can.

Ultimately. Do what works for you. And unfortunately there is a long way to go before A Beginner's Mess is accepted.
